hiroyuki-clips

A personal desktop tool that clips highlights from YouTube videos and posts them to TikTok.

Terms of Service

This application is a personal, non-commercial tool for private use only. By using this tool, you agree to comply with TikTok's Terms of Service and YouTube's Terms of Service. The developer is not responsible for any misuse.

Privacy Policy

This application does not collect, store, or share any personal data with third parties. Authentication tokens are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to any server other than TikTok's official API endpoints.

The app accesses TikTok APIs solely to upload video content on behalf of the authenticated user. No data is sold or shared.
